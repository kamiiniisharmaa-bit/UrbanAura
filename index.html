UrbanAura Collective wasn’t born from a factory — it emerged from a small atelier in the portuguese countryside, where potters, weavers and perfumers shape timeless pieces. Each object carries the mark of its maker and a quiet intention to last generations.

We partner with small-batch artisans across Europe and Japan, celebrating imperfections and raw textures. Our name reflects the duality: urban edge + earthy aura.
