An atelier for the curious & the mindful.

UrbanAura Collective exists at the intersection of raw craft and modern poetry — a gathering of objects that carry whispers of their makers. We don’t just sell things; we share stories, texture, and the quiet beauty of wabi-sabi.

Based between Lisbon, Kyoto, and Brooklyn, our collective champions slow production, timeless design, and a deep respect for natural materials.

We believe that every object should feel like an echo — of the earth, the hand, and a story worth telling.

— Manifesto of UrbanAura Collective

Our foundations
🌱

Radical slowness

We reject mass production. From clay to loom, each piece is made in limited series, often taking weeks to complete.

🤝

Maker-first ethics

Fair wages, transparent sourcing, and long-term relationships with artisans across Portugal, Japan and Morocco.

🍂

Imperfection as identity

We celebrate kintsugi philosophy — cracks, color variations, and organic edges are marks of authenticity.

🌀

Circular future

Repair kits, take-back programs, and plastic-free shipping are core to our 2030 low-waste pledge.

The atelier in Lisbon’s Alfama

Behind a cobalt-blue door, our flagship space doubles as a gallery, workshop, and meeting place for fellow slow makers. You’ll find shelves of unfired clay, looms humming, and a small shop where every piece has a tag signed by its maker.

Since 2021, we have hosted over 40 intimate workshops — from natural dyeing to hand-building ceramics. We invite you to visit by appointment or follow our open-studio days.
