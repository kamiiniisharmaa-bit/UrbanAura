Privacy & data ethics

At UrbanAura Collective, we honor your privacy as thoughtfully as we craft our objects. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our site, shop, or newsletter.

1. Information we collect

UrbanAura Collective collects only the data necessary to provide a seamless, personalized experience. This includes:

  • Personal identifiers — name, email address, billing/shipping address, phone number (when you create an account or place an order).
  • Transaction data — purchase history, payment method details (handled by PCI-compliant processors; we do not store full card numbers).
  • Technical information — IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and browsing behavior via analytics tools.
  • Communication data — messages you send to our support team or via the newsletter signup form.

We never knowingly collect sensitive data (e.g., health, political opinions) unless you explicitly provide it in correspondence.

2. How we use your data

Your information helps us build a better atelier — we use it for:

  • Processing and fulfilling orders, including shipping updates and returns.
  • Sending you the "Aura Dispatch" newsletter (only if you opt in). You may unsubscribe at any time.
  • Improving our website, product recommendations, and customer service.
  • Preventing fraud, verifying eligibility, and complying with legal obligations.
  • Personalizing your experience — we may show you relevant objects based on past views.
✦ Our promise: We never sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

3. Cookies & similar technologies

We use essential, functional, and analytical cookies to improve navigation and understand how visitors explore our collections. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings, but disabling essential cookies may affect site features.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential: cart, checkout, security.
  • Preference: remember your language or currency.
  • Analytics: privacy-friendly statistics (via aggregated data).

Third-party services (like payment gateways or shipping) may set their own cookies; we recommend reviewing their policies.

4. Sharing & third parties

We only share your data with trusted partners who help us run UrbanAura Collective, under strict confidentiality agreements:

  • Fulfillment partners: carriers (e.g., DHL, local postal services) and warehouses.
  • Payment processors: Stripe, PayPal — they handle payment data in compliance with PCI DSS.
  • IT & analytics: secure cloud hosting, email marketing platform (Klaviyo / similar), and limited analytics tools.

We may disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, or in case of a business transfer (merger/acquisition). In such events, you will be notified.

5. Data security

We implement industry-standard measures: SSL encryption, firewalls, role-based access, and regular security audits. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to protect your account credentials and contact us immediately if you suspect any breach.

All payment transactions are tokenized and processed via secure third-party gateways — UrbanAura does not store full card details.

6. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location (EEA, UK, California, etc.), you may have the following rights:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erasure (right to be forgotten): ask us to delete your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Restriction & portability: limit processing or receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of marketing: click "unsubscribe" in any newsletter or email us.

To exercise your rights, contact our privacy team at privacy@urbanaura.co. We will respond within 30 days and never discriminate against you for using these rights.

7. Data retention

We keep your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by tax, legal, or accounting regulations (e.g., order records for 7 years in some jurisdictions).

Newsletter data is retained until you unsubscribe. Inactive accounts may be anonymized after 24 months.

8. Children's privacy

Our site is not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it.

9. International data transfers

UrbanAura Collective operates from Portugal (EU) and serves customers worldwide. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws. When we transfer data outside the EEA or UK, we ensure adequate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent protections.

10. Updates to this policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Effective date" at the top indicates the latest version. Significant changes will be announced via our website or email notification. Continued use of the site after changes implies acceptance of the revised policy.
